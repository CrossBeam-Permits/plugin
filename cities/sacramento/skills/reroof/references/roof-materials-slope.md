## Asphalt Shingles (CBC 1507.2 / CRC R905.2)

### Material Standard
- ASTM D3462

### Slope Requirements
- **Minimum 2:12** (2 units rise per 12 units run)
- Slopes 2:12 to 4:12: Double underlayment required
- Slopes 4:12 and steeper: Single underlayment acceptable

### Underlayment
Per wind zone (Table 1507.1.1 / Table R905.1.1):

| Wind Condition | Acceptable Standards |
|---|---|
| Low wind (V < 140 mph) | ASTM D226 Type I or II, D1970, D4869 Types I–IV |
| High wind (V ≥ 140 mph) | ASTM D226 Type II, D1970, D4869 Types III–IV |

Fastening: Grid pattern — 12" between side laps, 6" at end/side laps.

### Fasteners
- Galvanized, stainless steel, aluminum, or copper nails
- Minimum 12-gage (0.105" shank), 3/8" diameter head
- Penetration: ≥3/4" through roof sheathing
- Minimum 4 fasteners per strip shingle OR 2 per individual shingle

### Wind Resistance
- Must comply with ASTM D7158 or D3161
- Classification D, G, or H depending on design wind speed

## Clay and Concrete Tile (CBC 1507.3 / CRC R905.3)

### Material Standards
- Clay tile: ASTM C1167
- Concrete tile: ASTM C1492

### Slope Requirements
- **Minimum 2.5:12** (21% slope)
- Slopes 2.5:12 to 4:12: Double underlayment required

### Underlayment
- ASTM D226 Type II, D2626, or D6380 Class M
- Deck must be solid sheathing (exception: spaced lumber in Seismic Categories A, B, C)

### Fasteners (Table 1507.3.7)
- 1–2 fasteners per tile depending on wind speed and building height
- ≥11-gage, 5/16" head minimum
- Penetration: 3/4" into deck or through thickness (whichever less)

### Valley Flashing
- Extend 11" from centerline on each side
- Splash diverter ≥1" high at valley centerline

## Metal Roof Panels (CBC 1507.4)

### Slope Requirements
- Standing seam: **0.25:12** (2%)
- Lapped with sealant: **0.5:12** (4%)
- Lapped without sealant: **3:12** (25%)

### Material Standards (Table 1507.4.3)

| Metal | Standard | Min Thickness |
|---|---|---|
| Aluminum (roll-formed) | ASTM B209 | 0.024" |
| Aluminum (shingles) | ASTM B209 | 0.019" |
| Galvanized steel | ASTM A653 G-90 | — |
| Copper (sheets) | — | 16 oz/sq ft |
| Copper (shingles) | — | 12 oz/sq ft |
| Stainless steel | ASTM A240 (300 Series) | — |
| Aluminum-zinc alloy | ASTM A792 AZ50 | — |

### Fasteners
- Per manufacturer specifications
- Galvanic compatibility required (e.g., copper roofing → copper/brass/bronze/stainless fasteners)

## Flat Roof Systems

### Built-Up Roofing (CBC 1507.10)
- **Min slope:** 0.25:12 (asphalt), 0.125:12 (coal-tar)
- Materials per Table 1507.10.2: ASTM D226, D2178, D4601 (felts); D2822, D3019, D4586 (cements); D1863 (aggregate)

### Modified Bitumen (CBC 1507.11)
- **Min slope:** 0.25:12
- Standards: ASTM D6162, D6163, D6164, D6222, D6223, D6298, D6509
- Base sheet: ASTM D1970 or D4601 permitted under cap sheet

### Single-Ply (CBC 1507.12)
- **Min slope:** 0.25:12
- EPDM: ASTM D4637
- PVC: ASTM D4434
- TPO: ASTM D6878
- KEE: ASTM D6754
- Ballasted systems: per ANSI/SPRI RP-4

### Sprayed Polyurethane Foam (CBC 1507.13)
- **Min slope:** 0.25:12
- Material: ASTM C1029 Type III/IV or D7425
- Protective coating: applied 2–72 hours post-application (acrylic, silicone, or moisture-cured polyurethane)

| Standard | Type | Common Use |
|---|---|---|
| ASTM D226 Type I | Asphalt-saturated felt (15#) | Low-wind shingles |
| ASTM D226 Type II | Asphalt-saturated felt (30#) | High-wind, tile |
| ASTM D1970 | Self-adhering polymer-modified bitumen | All zones, versatile |
| ASTM D4869 Types I–IV | Asphalt-coated glass fiber | Types III–IV for high wind |
| ASTM D2626 | Asphalt-saturated/coated glass fiber | Tile applications |
| ASTM D6380 Class M | Mineral-surfaced | Tile applications |

**Note:** Ice barrier (ASTM D1970 at eaves) is NOT required in most of Southern California — no snow load concern. Required only in areas where the average daily temperature in January is 25°F or less.

## Flashing and Drainage (CBC 1503 / CRC R903)

### Required Flashing Locations (CBC 1503.2)
- Wall-roof intersections
- Gutters
- Changes in roof slope or direction
- Around roof openings and penetrations

### Metal Flashing Specifications
- Minimum thickness: **0.019"** (No. 26 galvanized sheet)
- Must be corrosion-resistant
- Specific metals per Table 1507.2.8.2:

| Metal | Min Thickness | Standard/Weight |
|---|---|---|
| Copper | 0.0216" | 16 oz/sq ft, ASTM B370 |
| Galvanized steel | 0.0179" | 26 gage (G90) |
| Aluminum | 0.024" | — |
| Stainless steel | 28 gage | — |
| Lead | — | 2.5 lbs |

### Crickets and Saddles (CBC 1503.5 / CRC R903.2.2)
- Required for any chimney or penetration **>30" wide** perpendicular to slope
- Material: Sheet metal or same covering as roof

### Drainage (CRC R903.4)
- Roof drainage must NOT discharge onto adjacent property
- Overflow drainage required for parapet roofs
- Valley flashing required at slope intersections
