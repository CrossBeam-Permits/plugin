## Plan-Check Checklist (applies to both modes)

Every item here is verified at plan-check time. Severity drives reviewer-mode output.

| # | Check | Severity | What to verify | Code |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Equipment location valid?** | ⚠️ **flag** | Not in prohibited space (bedroom/bathroom for gas); proper access, passageway, platform per installation type | CMC 904.1, 304.4, 305.1 |
| 3 | **System properly sized?** | ❌ **fail** | Load calc per ACCA Manual J; equipment selected per Manual S; capacity within limits | Energy Code 150.0(h) |
| 4 | **Equipment efficiency meets minimum?** | ❌ **fail** | SEER2/HSPF2/AFUE meets federal minimums | Energy Code 110.1 |
| 5 | **Thermostat compliant?** | ⚠️ **flag** | Programmable setback thermostat (4 periods/24 hr); heat pump supplementary heater controls | Energy Code 110.2(c), 110.2(b) |
| 2 | **Manual J Load Calculation** `[YMC]` | ⚠️ **flag** | For new HVAC installations or systems being resized, a Manual J (or equivalent ACCA-approved method) load calculation is required. Like-for-like replacements at same tonnage/capacity may qualify for OTC without full Manual J, but oversizing is a flag. | ACCA Manual J; Title 24 Part 6 |
| 3 | **Duct Leakage Test — > 40 ft Trigger** `[YMC]` | ⚠️ **flag** | If more than 40 linear feet of ductwork is being replaced or newly installed, a **post-installation duct leakage test** is required per Title 24 Part 6. The test must be performed by a HERS rater. If ≤ 40 ft of duct is replaced, leakage testing is not triggered. Document the linear feet of duct replaced in the permit application. | 2022 Title 24 Part 6 §150.2(b) |
| 4 | **Like-for-Like OTC Path** `[YMC]` | ⚠️ **flag** | Standard like-for-like HVAC replacement (same location, same or equivalent tonnage, no new ductwork) is typically handled OTC at Yucaipa Building & Safety on Wednesdays or submitted online via Tyler EnerGov. Equipment cut sheet and installation detail required even for OTC permits. | CITY.md |
| 5 | **Simultaneous Weatherization — Re-sizing Required** `[YMC]` | ⚠️ **flag** | If the home is also undergoing significant building envelope improvements (new insulation, window upgrades, air sealing) at the same time as HVAC replacement, the HVAC system may need to be re-sized to reflect the reduced heating/cooling loads. An updated Manual J is required to avoid oversizing, which reduces system efficiency and occupant comfort. | ACCA Manual J; Title 24 Part 6 |
| 6 | **SCE / SoCalGas Utilities — No Special Program Fee** `[YMC]` | ⚠️ **flag** | Yucaipa is served by SCE (electric) and SoCalGas (gas). No special utility interconnection fee or utility program requirement affects the building permit path. Applicants may separately apply for SCE/SoCalGas rebate programs for high-efficiency units. | CITY.md |
| 7 | **Heat Pump / All-Electric HVAC** `[YMC]` | ⚠️ **flag** | For heat pump or all-electric HVAC installations, confirm SCE service capacity and panel capacity. Heat pumps have higher electrical demand than gas furnaces — include load calculation and panel schedule in the submittal. | CEC; Title 24 |
| 8 | **Submit via EnerGov Portal or OTC Wednesday** `[YMC]` | ⚠️ **flag** | Use Tyler EnerGov "Yucaipa Permit Exchange" portal for online submittal. OTC intake available Wednesdays 1:00–4:30 PM only. | CITY.md |

## Deferred to Inspection

|---|---|---|---|
| 2 | **Clearance to combustibles OK?** | Per manufacturer's instructions; closet/alcove volume rule; noncombustible floor | CMC 303.1, 303.2, 904.3 |
| 11 | **Electrical disconnect provided?** | Disconnect in sight of equipment; dedicated circuit for furnace; 120V receptacle within 25 ft | CMC 301.4; CEC 422.10, 440.14 |
| 12 | **Condensate drain installed?** | Primary drain with 1/8 in./ft slope; overflow protection (pan, switch, or secondary drain) where damage could result | CMC 310.1, 310.2 |
| 14 | **A/C electrical requirements met?** | Disconnect rating min 115% of nameplate rated-load current or branch-circuit selection current (440.12(A)(1)); disconnect within sight and readily accessible from equipment, not on access panels or obscuring nameplates (440.14); branch circuit conductor sizing per compressor nameplate (440.32, 440.33); motor controller disconnect accessible (440.13); A/C equipment anchored/secured including seismic (CEC 440.12, CMC 303.4). | CEC 440.12, 440.13, 440.14, 440.32, 440.33; CMC 303.4 |
