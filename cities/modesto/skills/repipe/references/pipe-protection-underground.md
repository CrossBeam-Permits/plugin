# CPC Pipe Protection & Underground Installation

Source: 2025 California Plumbing Code (Title 24, Part 5), Chapters 3 and 6.

---

## A. Pipe Protection Through Framing (312.9)

Steel nail plates are required for plastic piping or tubing, and copper or copper alloy piping or tubing that penetrates framing members to within 1 inch of the exposed framing face.

| Requirement | Detail | Code Section |
|-------------|--------|-------------|
| **When required** | Pipes OTHER THAN cast iron or galvanized steel in notches or holes less than 1-1/4 in. to face of framing | CPC 312.9 |
| **Plate gauge** | Minimum No. 18 gauge (0.0478 in. / 1.2 mm) | CPC 312.9 |
| **Plate extent** | Shall extend not less than 1-1/2 in. beyond the outside diameter of the pipe | CPC 312.9 |
| **Sole/top plates** | Plates shall extend a minimum of 2 in. above and below sole and top plates | CPC 312.9 |

---

## B. Underground Installation — Trenching & Backfill (314.1, 314.4)

| Requirement | Detail | Code Section |
|-------------|--------|-------------|
| **Bearing support** | Smooth continuous bearing support for pipes in trench | CPC 314.1 |
| **Firm bed** | Pipe shall be supported on firm bed; no contact with sharp objects | CPC 314.4 |
| **First 12 in. backfill** | No rocks or debris in the first 12 in. of backfill over pipe | CPC 314.4 |
| **Backfill layers** | Thin layers (max 6 in.) tamped in place | CPC 314.4 |
| **Heavy equipment** | No backhoe or grader until 12 in. of earth tamped over pipe | CPC 314.4.1 |
| **Parallel trenches** | Shall not run under footings, nor within the area 45 degrees from vertical under footings | CPC 314.1 |

---

## C. Water Service Depth & Tracer Wire (609.1, 604.10.1)

| Requirement | Detail | Code Section |
|-------------|--------|-------------|
| **Minimum depth** | Water service shall be minimum 12 in. below finished grade | CPC 609.1 |
| **Frost line** | Water pipe shall be minimum 6 in. below frost line | CPC 609.1 |
| **Plastic tracer wire** | Plastic underground water service shall have #14 blue-insulated tracer wire terminating above ground at each end | CPC 604.10.1 |

---

## D. Piping in Common Trench (609.2)

Water and sewer shall NOT be in the same trench UNLESS both of the following conditions are met:

1. The bottom of the water pipe shall be not less than **12 inches above the top** of the sewer or drain line.
2. The water pipe shall be placed on a **solid shelf excavated at one side** of the common trench with a clear horizontal distance of not less than **12 inches** from the sewer or drain line.

---

## E. Pipes Parallel to Footings (609.3, 314.1)

Pipes shall NOT be installed within the area **45 degrees from vertical** under footings to prevent structural load on pipes.

---

## F. Piping in Concrete or Masonry (312.2, 312.3, 312.10)

| Requirement | Detail | Code Section |
|-------------|--------|-------------|
| **No direct embedding** | Piping shall not be directly embedded in concrete or masonry | CPC 312.2 |
| **Corrosive soil** | Protective sheath for piping embedded in corrosive soil — min. 8 mil plastic, allows movement | CPC 312.3 |
| **Sleeves through foundation** | Sleeve required for pipe penetrations through foundation walls or under footings | CPC 312.10.1 |
| **Sleeve exception** | Sleeve not required for bored or drilled openings | CPC 312.10.2 |
| **Sleeve size** | Minimum 2 sizes greater than the pipe running through foundation | CPC 312.10.1 |
| **Annular space** | Shall be sealed between pipes and sleeves | CPC 312.10.2 |

---

## G. Piping Under Concrete Slabs (609.3)

| Requirement | Detail | Code Section |
|-------------|--------|-------------|
| **Galvanized prohibited** | Galvanized piping shall NOT be permitted under concrete slabs | CPC 609.3(1) |
| **Copper joints** | Copper pipe shall be installed without joints where possible | CPC 609.3(2) |
| **Copper brazing** | Copper pipe joints under slab shall be BRAZED (not soldered), with wrought-copper fittings | CPC 609.3(2) |
| **Copper type** | Copper pipe underground beneath a building shall be minimum Type L | CPC 604.3 |
| **Pressure test** | All piping shall be pressure tested before casting concrete | CPC 609.4 / 105.1 |

---

## H. Pressure Testing (609.4)

| Parameter | Requirement | Code Section |
|-----------|------------|-------------|
| **Test method** | Water or air test (plastic pipe: water only, unless manufacturer permits air for PEX/PP/PE-RT) | CPC 609.4 |
| **Test pressure** | Not less than working pressure and not less than 50 psi (for air) | CPC 609.4 |
| **Duration** | Held for not less than 15 minutes with no evidence of leakage | CPC 609.4 |
