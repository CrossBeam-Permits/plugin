# State Fire Marshal Retail License & Local Permit Requirements

## State Fire Marshal Retail License

### HSC 12574 — Retail Sales License

A retail sales license allows the retail sale of safe and sane fireworks for private use. This license is issued by the **State Fire Marshal** (Office of the State Fire Marshal / CAL FIRE).

### HSC 12599 — License Period and Application Deadline

A retail license authorizes retail sale of safe and sane fireworks **only during the period of 12:00 noon on June 28 through 12:00 noon on July 6** of the same calendar year. The license expires at the end of that period.

**Application deadline**: No retail license shall be issued unless the application is received by the State Fire Marshal **on or before June 15** preceding the license period.

A new retail sales license is required **annually**.

### HSC 12600 — Non-Transferability

License authority is limited to the licensee and **shall not be transferable**. The person or organization named on the license is the only entity authorized to sell under it.

### State License Fee — Title 19 CCR Section 981.3

- **Retail license fee: $50** (payable to CDF / State Fire Marshal)
- Non-refundable once issued
- Fiscal year runs July 1 through June 30, except retail licenses operate noon June 28 through noon July 6

## Age Restrictions

### HSC 12583 — Seller Age

No person under the age of **18** shall sell, or handle for sale, any classification of fireworks.

**Registration**: Retail sales personnel do **not** require registration with the State Fire Marshal. This is different from wholesale and manufacturing personnel, who do require registration.

## Local Permit Mandate

The state requires that cities/counties also issue their own local permits in addition to the state license.

### HSC 12640 — Local Permit Required

In addition to the state license, licensees must possess a **valid permit from the local fire authority** before selling safe and sane fireworks at retail. No person shall sell fireworks at retail without both the state license AND the local permit.

### HSC 12643 — Application Timeline

Written applications for local permits must be submitted at least **10 days prior** to the proposed sales activity, to the chief of the fire department or chief fire prevention officer of the jurisdiction.

### HSC 12644 — Evidence of State License

Applicants must present evidence of a valid **state retail license** at the time of local permit application. The local authority should verify the state license is current and covers the applicant organization.

### HSC 12645 — Investigation

The investigating officer (fire marshal, fire inspector, or designee) shall examine the proposed location and investigate the applicant. Findings and recommendations are submitted to the city/county governing body.

### HSC 12646 — Approval Authority

The governing body of the city or county may **grant or deny** the permit and may impose **reasonable conditions** on the permit. Conditions may relate to location, hours, safety measures, insurance, and other operational requirements.

### HSC 12647 — Delegation

Governing bodies may delegate permit authority to issuing officers. In many cities, this is the Fire Chief, Building Official, or Director of Community Development.

## Local Fee Authority

A city that authorizes safe and sane fireworks sales can require each applicant to pay a fee covering a **pro rata portion of actual and reasonable costs** for:
- Processing permits and applications
- Conducting inspections
- Public awareness campaigns about fireworks safety
- Fire suppression efforts during the fireworks season

**Cap**: This fee **cannot exceed 7% of gross sales** of fireworks sold during the applicable license period.

## Key Deadlines Summary

| Deadline | Requirement | Authority |
|----------|-------------|-----------|
| **June 15** | State Fire Marshal license application received | HSC 12599 |
| **10 days prior** | Local fire authority permit application filed | HSC 12643 |
| **Noon June 28** | Earliest sales can begin (state window opens) | HSC 12599 |
| **Noon July 6** | Latest sales can occur (state window closes) | HSC 12599 |
| **City-specific** | City may set earlier deadlines, narrower windows | Per local ordinance |
