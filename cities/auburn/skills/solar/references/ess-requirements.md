# Energy Storage System (ESS) Requirements

Source: 2025 California Residential Code (Title 24, Part 2.5), Section R328; 2025 California Fire Code (Title 24, Part 9), Section 1207; 2025 California Electrical Code (Title 24, Part 3), Article 706.

---

## General (CRC R328)

ESS shall be listed and labeled per **UL 9540**.

**Exception**: Where approved, repurposed unlisted battery systems from electric vehicles are allowed to be installed **outdoors** or in **detached sheds** located not less than **5 ft (1524 mm)** from exterior walls, property lines, and public ways.

---

## Installation Requirements (CRC R328.3)

- Installed per manufacturer instructions and listing
- **Spacing**: Individual units separated by not less than **3 ft (914 mm)** from each other
  - Exception: Smaller separation permitted where documented adequate by large-scale fire testing per CFC 1207.1.5

---

## Permitted Locations (CRC R328.4)

ESS shall be installed ONLY in:

| Location | Notes |
|----------|-------|
| **Detached garages and detached accessory structures** | -- |
| **Attached garages** | Separated from dwelling per R302.6 |
| **Outdoors / exterior side of exterior walls** | Not less than **3 ft (914 mm) from doors and windows** directly entering dwelling |
| **Enclosed utility closets, basements, storage/utility spaces** within dwelling | Finished or noncombustible walls and ceilings required. Unfinished wood-frame: provide **5/8" Type X gypsum wallboard** |

### Locations NOT Permitted
- **Sleeping rooms**
- Closets or spaces **opening directly into sleeping rooms**
- **Habitable spaces** of dwelling units

---

## Maximum Aggregate Ratings (CRC R328.5 / Table R328.5)

| Location | Max Aggregate kWh | Installation Requirements |
|----------|-------------------|--------------------------|
| Utility closets, basements, storage/utility spaces in dwellings | **40** | -- |
| Attached garages | **80** | -- |
| Exterior walls within 3 ft of dwellings (noncombustible wall) | **200** | Exterior walls/eaves constructed with noncombustible surface* |
| Exterior walls within 3 ft (no noncombustible) | **100** | -- |
| On/within 3 ft of exterior walls, combustible | **100** | -- |
| Detached garages/accessory structures (<10 ft from dwellings) | **200** | -- |
| Detached garages/accessory structures (>=10 ft from dwellings) | **600** | -- |
| Outdoors on ground (<10 ft from property lines/dwellings) | **200** | Min 3 ft from property lines and dwellings |
| Outdoors on ground (>=10 ft from property lines/dwellings) | **600** | Min 10 ft from property lines and dwellings |

*Noncombustible wall surface shall extend: min 5 ft horizontally from ESS edge, min 1 ft below bottom edge, min 8 ft above ESS or to noncombustible eave (whichever less).*

### Individual Unit Limits
- Individual ESS units: max **20 kWh** each
- Total aggregate on property: max **600 kWh**
- Installations exceeding limits: per CFC 1207.1 through 1207.9

---

## Electrical Requirements (CRC R328.6)

- Installed per **California Electrical Code (Article 706)**
- Inverters listed per **UL 1741** or provided as part of UL 9540 listing
- Grid-connected systems: inverters listed for **utility interaction**

---

## Smoke and Heat Alarms (CRC R328.7)

### Smoke Alarms
Rooms and areas where ESS installed (dwelling units, basements, attached garages) shall have **smoke alarms per R314**.

### Heat Detectors
A **listed heat detector**, interconnected to smoke alarms, shall be installed in locations where smoke alarms **cannot be installed based on their listing** (e.g., garages).

**Note**: Attached garages with ESS require a listed heat detector interconnected with dwelling smoke alarms.

---

## Impact Protection (CRC R328.8)

### When Required
ESS installed in locations subject to **vehicle damage**:

### Normal Driving Path (R328.8.1)
Space between garage vehicle opening and interior face of back wall, to 48" above finished floor, width of garage door opening.

Impact protection required for ESS:
- In the normal driving path
- On back wall within **36" left or right** of driving path
- On side wall within **24" from back wall** and **36" of driving path**

**Exception**: ESS installed **>=48" above finished floor** in driving path on back wall, or **>=36" AFF** on side wall, do not require impact protection.

### Impact Protection Methods (R328.8.3)
- **Bollards**: 3" diameter Schedule 80 iron pipe, 38" high, 6" min diameter, 12" min embedment
- **Wheel barriers**: Approved method
- **Other approved methods**

### Bollard Notes
- Bollards **NOT required** for side wall mounted ESS >=36" AFF
- Bollards **NOT required** for back wall mounted ESS >=48" AFF in driving path
- Bollards **NOT allowed** in required garage parking spaces (10'x20' or 20'x20')
- Wall-mounted ESS: max **10" encroachment** into required parking clearance, mounted min 48" AFF in driving path

---

## Mechanical Ventilation (CRC R328.9)

ESS that produce **hydrogen or other flammable gases** during charging shall be provided with **mechanical ventilation** per California Mechanical Code.

**Note**: Lithium-ion batteries do not typically produce hydrogen during normal operation. Lead-acid and nickel-cadmium batteries may require ventilation.

---

## Vehicle-to-Home (CRC R328.10)

Temporary use of owner/occupant electric vehicle to power dwelling while parked in attached/detached garage or outdoors:
- Comply with **vehicle manufacturer instructions**
- Comply with **California Electrical Code**

---

## Labeling and Documentation (CRC R328.11)

1. Copy of manufacturer's **installation, operation, maintenance, and decommissioning instructions** provided to owner or placed at conspicuous location near ESS
2. **Label on installed system** with contact information for qualified maintenance and service providers

---

## ESS Exceeding Table R328.5 Limits (CRC R328.12)

ESS with capacities exceeding Table R328.5 values or Table 1207.1.1 (CFC) shall comply with **CFC Section 1207.1 through 1207.9**.

---

## Toxic Gas Prohibition (CRC R328.13)

ESS that have potential to release **toxic or highly toxic gas** during charging, discharging, and normal use shall **NOT be installed** within Group R-3 or R-4 occupancies.
