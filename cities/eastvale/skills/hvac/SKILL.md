| # | Check | Severity | What to verify | Code |

| 1 | **Equipment location valid?** | ⚠️ **flag** | Not in prohibited space (bedroom/bathroom for gas); proper access, passageway, platform per installation type | CMC 904.1, 304.4, 305.1 |
| 3 | **System properly sized?** | ❌ **fail** | Load calc per ACCA Manual J; equipment selected per Manual S; capacity within limits | Energy Code 150.0(h) |
| 4 | **Equipment efficiency meets minimum?** | ❌ **fail** | SEER2/HSPF2/AFUE meets federal minimums | Energy Code 110.1 |
| 5 | **Thermostat compliant?** | ⚠️ **flag** | Programmable setback thermostat (4 periods/24 hr); heat pump supplementary heater controls | Energy Code 110.2(c), 110.2(b) |
| 3 | **Heat pump — no gas backup** `[EMC]` | ⚠️ **flag** | Eastvale follows state policy disfavoring new gas infrastructure in new construction. For new HVAC installations in new construction, verify no new gas furnace is being installed as primary heat in applications that could use heat pump. This is a state policy direction, not a hard Eastvale ordinance, but note for permit records. | Energy Code 150.0(h) note; state policy |
| 4 | **SoCalGas service (gas systems)** `[EMC]` | ⚠️ **flag** | SoCalGas serves Eastvale. For gas appliance replacements, verify the existing gas line is adequate for the new appliance BTU input. | 7 | **Refrigerant charge verification** `[EMC]` | ⚠️ **flag** | Required for all cooling equipment replacements in California. Standard charge per manufacturer OR weigh-in procedure. | 9 | **Contractor licensing** `[EMC]` | ⚠️ **flag** | Licensed C-20 (HVAC) or C-36 (Plumbing, for hydronic/gas work) contractor required. C-10 (Electrical) for electrical work. Current CSLB license + Eastvale city business license. | CMC 301; H&S Code 1900 et seq. |
| 10 | **Permit portal** `[EMC]` | ⚠️ **flag** | Submit through Eastvale Permit Portal at eastvaleca.gov. Building Safety Division: (951) 703-4450. ## Deferred to Inspection

These items are verified by the inspector in the field — **not** part of plan-check corrections. The reviewer should NOT flag these as corrections to the applicant. Inspector responsibilities:

| # | Inspector verifies | What to look for | Code |
|---|---|---|---|
| 2 | **Clearance to combustibles OK?** | Per manufacturer's instructions; closet/alcove volume rule; noncombustible floor | CMC 303.1, 303.2, 904.3 |
| 11 | **Electrical disconnect provided?** | Disconnect in sight of equipment; dedicated circuit for furnace; 120V receptacle within 25 ft | CMC 301.4; CEC 422.10, 440.14 |
| 12 | **Condensate drain installed?** | Primary drain with 1/8 in./ft slope; overflow protection (pan, switch, or secondary drain) where damage could result | CMC 310.1, 310.2 |
| 14 | **A/C electrical requirements met?** | Disconnect rating min 115% of nameplate rated-load current or branch-circuit selection current (440.12(A)(1)); disconnect within sight and readily accessible from equipment, not on access panels or obscuring nameplates (440.14); branch circuit conductor sizing per compressor nameplate (440.32, 440.33); motor controller disconnect accessible (440.13); A/C equipment anchored/secured including seismic (CEC 440.12, CMC 303.4). | CEC 440.12, 440.13, 440.14, 440.32, 440.33; CMC 303.4 |
| 15 | **Smoke and CO alarms compliant?** | Smoke alarms in all required locations (retrofit on permitted work); CO alarms where fuel appliances exist | CRC R310, R311 |
