## Plan-Check Checklist (applies to both modes)

Every item here is verified at plan-check time. Severity drives reviewer-mode output.

| # | Check | Severity | What to verify | Code |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Permit type correct?** | ❌ **fail** | Pool/spa demolition permit; partial demo (shell remains below grade) for landscape use only. If structure will be built over the area, full removal with geotech report is required -- different permit process. | CBC 105.1 |
| 2 | **Site plan complete?** | ❌ **fail** | Property lines with dimensions, all buildings/structures shown, pool/spa location, easements/setbacks, driveways, streets, north arrow, fence locations with heights, electrical panel and gas meter locations, proposed fill material specified, debris/backfill storage location, erosion control measures. | CBC 107.2.6 |
| 3 | **Scope of work defined?** | ❌ **fail** | Permit is for demolition of existing pool/spa including capping of associated plumbing and electrical. No other improvements allowed under this permit. | CBC 3303.1 |
| 4 | **Pool dimensions provided?** | ⚠️ **flag** | Length, width, deepest depth, shallowest depth, estimated volume in gallons. These determine backfill quantity and inspection scope. | -- |
| 5 | **Drainage holes adequate?** | ❌ **fail** | Pool: 2 holes at 36" dia, one at lowest point. Spa: 1 hole at 36" dia at lowest point. Holes equally spaced across deep end and levels. | CBC 3303.5 |
| 6 | **Gravel layer specified?** | ❌ **fail** | 12-inch layer of #4 rock (1" to 2.5" Class II permeable free-draining rock) over drain holes and pool shell bottom. | CBC 3303.5 |
| 7 | **Coping/bond beam removal adequate?** | ❌ **fail** | Remove pool coping, bond beam, and steel rebar around entire perimeter to at least 18 inches below finished grade. | CBC 3303.4 |
| 8 | **Backfill material specified?** | ❌ **fail** | Self-compacting fill sand (SE25 type), clean fill dirt, or as specified in soils report. No organic material, construction debris, cobbles, or boulders. Fill to within 30 inches of final surface, then planting medium for upper 18 inches. | CBC 1804.3, J107.4 |
| 9 | **Compaction method specified?** | ❌ **fail** | Hand compaction equipment, 6-inch lifts, prudent use of water. 90% minimum (90-95% typical) per ASTM D1557 Modified Proctor. Soil compaction report required at final inspection. | CBC 1804.6, 1705.6, J107.5 |
| 10 | **Geotechnical filter fabric?** | ⚠️ **flag** | Filter fabric applied after backfilling, before planting medium layer. Separates structural backfill from planting medium. | -- |
| 16 | **Pool water discharge plan?** | ❌ **fail** | Water must be de-chlorinated before discharge to storm drain. Chlorinated/contaminated water must go off-site. No filter backwash or cleaning wastewater to storm drain. | NPDES/BMP |
| 17 | **Drainage/grading adequate?** | ❌ **fail** | Finished grade slopes minimum 2% away from all existing structures. Drainage pattern does not increase runoff to neighboring properties. | CBC 1804.4 |
| 18 | **Erosion control measures?** | ❌ **fail** | BMP measures during construction: silt fencing, gravel bags at storm drains, covered stockpiles. Erosion control on finished slopes before final inspection. | CBC 3304.1.5, J110.1 |
| 1 | **Water District Confirmation — WMWD vs. JCSD** `[EMC]` | ⚠️ **flag** | Before advising on water line capping: confirm which water district serves the parcel. Eastvale is split between WMWD (most parcels west of Hamner Ave and in the core residential areas) and JCSD (portions of the eastern and older areas). Contact the applicable district for water service disconnection and capping requirements. Incorrect assumption of district can delay permit finaling. | WMWD (wmwd.com, 951-926-1100); JCSD (jcsd.us, 951-685-7434) |
| 2 | **SCE — Electrical Service Disconnect** `[EMC]` | ⚠️ **flag** | Pool sub-panel and all electrical circuits serving pool equipment (pump, lights, heater, chlorinator) must be disconnected and removed. Coordinate with Southern California Edison (SCE) if any modification to the main service entrance is required. SCE must be contacted early — service disconnect scheduling can add lead time. Electrical disconnect permit is separate from the pool demolition permit. | CEC 680.13; SCE (sce.com); CITY.md |
| 3 | **SoCalGas — Gas Line Cap** `[EMC]` | ⚠️ **flag** | If the pool has a gas heater: SoCalGas must be notified and the gas line must be capped at the meter AND at the heater location per CPC 1206.3. Obtain SoCalGas confirmation of gas shut-off and cap before demolition of heater equipment begins. SoCalGas will inspect the gas cap in many cases. | CPC 1206.3, 1210.8.1; SoCalGas (socalgas.com) |
| 4 | **SDC D — Backfill Compaction Report** `[EMC]` | ⚠️ **flag** | Eastvale is in Seismic Design Category D. A soil compaction report prepared by a licensed geotechnical engineer or special inspector is required at final inspection. In SDC D, under-compacted backfill presents liquefaction-related settlement risk. | CBC 1804.6, J107.5, 1705.6; CBC 1613; CITY.md |
| 5 | **No FHSZ — Demolition Not Affected by WUI** `[EMC]` | ⚠️ **flag** | Eastvale has no Fire Hazard Severity Zone designation. WUI construction requirements, defensible space obligations, and CAL FIRE demolition conditions do NOT apply to pool demolition in Eastvale. No fire plan check required for standard pool demolition permits. | CITY.md; OSFM FHSZ maps |
| 6 | **Pool Water Discharge — NPDES / WRCRWA** `[EMC]` | ⚠️ **flag** | Pool water must be de-chlorinated to below 0.1 ppm residual chlorine before discharge to the sanitary sewer or storm drain. Discharge of chlorinated water directly to the storm drain or to WRCRWA's sanitary sewer system is prohibited. Coordinate discharge plan with WRCRWA if connecting to sanitary sewer for discharge. | NPDES; WRCRWA; CPC (storm drain BMP) |
| 7 | **Grading Permit — Large Excavations** `[EMC]` | ⚠️ **flag** | If pool demolition includes full removal of the pool shell (not just fill/abandon-in-place), the resulting excavation and backfill volume may trigger a grading permit from the Eastvale Public Works or Community Development Department. | 8 | **Owner Disclosure and Landscaping Restriction** `[EMC]` | ⚠️ **flag** | Owner must sign disclosure that the backfilled area is for landscaping use only. | 9 | **Specific Plan Areas — Site Restoration** `[EMC]` | ⚠️ **flag** | For parcels in SP-R (The Ranch), SP-TE (The Enclave), SP-EV-300, or LEAL-MP: post-demolition landscaping of the pool area may be subject to HOA landscape standards or specific plan requirements.
