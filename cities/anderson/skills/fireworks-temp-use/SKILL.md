| 3 | **State license fee paid ($50)** | ❌ **fail** | $50 fee paid to CDF/State Fire Marshal | Title 19 CCR 981.3 |

| 5 | **All sellers are 18+** | ❌ **fail** | No person under 18 sells or handles fireworks for sale | HSC 12583 |
