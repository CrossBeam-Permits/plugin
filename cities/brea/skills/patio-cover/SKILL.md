## Plan-Check Checklist (applies to both modes)

Every item here is verified at plan-check time. Severity drives reviewer-mode output.

| # | Check | Severity | What to verify | Code |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Classification correct?** | ❌ **fail** | Confirm open (lattice) vs solid roof. Open covers must have at least 50% open area with min 1-1/2" spacing between shade bars. | CRC AH102, AH103 |
| 2 | **Height limit met?** | ❌ **fail** | Patio cover must not exceed 12 feet above average grade plane. One story only. | CRC AH101 |
| 3 | **Permitted use?** | ❌ **fail** | Recreational / outdoor living only. Not a carport, garage, storage room, or habitable room. | CRC AH102 |
| 4 | **Partial enclosure compliant?** | ❌ **fail** | If any walls are enclosed, the longer wall AND one additional wall must be at least 65% open. No framed windows. Insect screening or removable plastic up to 1/8" thick permitted. | CRC AH103 |
| 5 | **Footings sized correctly?** | ❌ **fail** | Footing size per Appendix H table based on tributary area. 12"x12" supports 100 sq ft (open) or 70 sq ft (solid). 18"x18" supports 220 sq ft (open) or 150 sq ft (solid). Minimum 12" deep. Concrete 3,000 psi. | CRC AH105, R403.1 |
| 6 | **Post size adequate?** | ❌ **fail** | Minimum 4x4 for lattice, 4x6 for solid roof. DF-L No. 2 or better. Pressure-treated where within 6" of ground or exposed to weather. | CRC R507.4, AH104 |
| 9 | **Knee braces provided?** | ❌ **fail** | Required at every post. 4x4 minimum (lattice) or 4x6 minimum (solid). Lag bolts at each end. Max tributary area per brace: 60 sq ft (open), 35 sq ft (solid light), 22 sq ft (solid heavy). | CRC AH104 |
| 10 | **Beam span within table?** | ❌ **fail** | Beam size adequate for span between posts and rafter span per beam span tables. | CRC R802, AH104 |
| 11 | **Rafter span within table?** | ❌ **fail** | Rafter size and spacing adequate for span from ledger (or beam) to beam per rafter span tables. | CRC R802, AH104 |
| 12 | **Rafter/beam overhang within limits?** | ❌ **fail** | Rafter overhang: max 6x rafter depth or 48". Beam overhang: max 1/3 of adjacent span or 48", and min 3' from property line. | CRC AH104 |
| 19 | **Materials compliant?** | ⚠️ **flag** | Wood: DF-L No. 2 or better, grade-marked. Concrete: 3,000 psi. Hardware: ICC-approved, installed per manufacturer specs with all recommended fasteners. | CRC AH104 |
| 20 | **Fire hazard zone?** | ❌ **fail** | Standard plans may not comply with WUI/Fuel Modification Zone requirements. Verify with local fire authority. Noncombustible construction may be required. | CRC R327 |
| 21 | **Engineering triggers?** | ❌ **fail** | Check for: height >12', spans exceeding tables, non-wood materials, high-wind zone, SDC D2/E, deviation from standard plan. If any apply, require stamped engineered plans. | CRC AH104 |
| 1 | **Height ≤ 15 ft verified** `[BMC]` | ⚠️ **flag** | Patio cover height must not exceed 15 ft for accessory structure classification — measure to highest point | BMC § 20.08 |
| 2 | **Attached vs. detached classification** `[BMC]` | ⚠️ **flag** | Confirm classification — attached covers subject to primary structure setbacks; detached covers subject to accessory structure setbacks and 15-ft height max | BMC Title 20 |
| 3 | **Setback compliance** `[BMC]` | ⚠️ **flag** | Verify setbacks per zoning district — plans must show distances to property lines, primary structure, and other structures | BMC Title 20 |
| 4 | **HFHSZ parcel check** `[BMC]` | ⚠️ **flag** | Verify parcel HFHSZ status — if in HFHSZ and cover has a solid roof, Class A roofing + CBC Ch. 7A ignition-resistant check required | BMC § 15.08.020; CBC 2025 Ch. 7A |
| 5 | **Solid vs. lattice roof determination** `[BMC]` | ⚠️ **flag** | Identify roof type on plans — solid roof triggers Class A requirement and HFHSZ Ch. 7A check; open lattice does not trigger Class A | BMC § 15.08.020 |
| 6 | **Class A roofing material specified** `[BMC]` | ⚠️ **flag** | For any solid-roof cover, plans must specify Class A roofing material — no non-classified or special-purpose roofing allowed per Brea local amendment | BMC § 15.08.020 / CBC § 1505.1.2 |
