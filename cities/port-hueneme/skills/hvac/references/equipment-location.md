# Equipment Location & Access

Source: 2025 California Mechanical Code (Title 24, Part 4), Chapters 3 and 9.

## Prohibited Locations (CMC 904.1)

Fuel-burning appliances shall NOT be installed in:
- Bedrooms
- Bathrooms
- Toilet rooms

> CRC reference: CRC 2406.2

## Clearance to Combustibles (CMC 303.1)

Clearance to combustibles shall be in accordance with the manufacturer's installation instructions.

### Clearance Reductions (CMC 303.2)

- Clearance reductions are NOT allowed for appliances listed for closet or alcove installation
- Central heating furnaces NOT listed for closet or alcove installation shall be installed in a room or space with a volume **not less than 12 times the total volume of the furnace**

## Anchoring (CMC 303.4)

All appliances shall be anchored in place to resist gravity loads, seismic forces, and wind forces.

---

## Rooftop Installations

### Access (CMC 304.3.1)

Buildings greater than 15 ft in height shall have an inside means of access to the roof for installation and service.

### Workspace (CMC 304.2)

Minimum 30 in. x 30 in. level workspace shall be provided on the service side of the appliance if the roof slope is greater than or equal to 4:12.

### Guardrail (CMC 304.3.1.1)

A minimum 42 in. tall guardrail shall be provided if the appliance is installed less than 10 ft from the roof edge.

---

## Garage Installations

### Impact Protection (CMC 305.1.1)

Appliances in garages shall be protected from impact by vehicles.

### Ignition Source Height (CMC 305.1)

Ignition source shall be a minimum 18 in. above the garage floor.

**Exception**: Appliances labeled as Flammable Vapor Ignition Resistant (FVIR).

### Separate Space Option (CMC 305.1.2)

Gas-fired appliances may be installed on the garage floor if located in a separate space with:
- Access only from outside the garage
- Exterior combustion air

---

## Attic Installations

### Access Opening (CMC 304.4)

Appliance shall be accessible through an opening and passageway not less than the **largest component of the appliance**, and not less than **22 in. x 30 in.**

### Passageway (CMC 304.4.1)

Maximum distance shall be **20 ft** from the access opening to the appliance where the height of the passageway is less than 6 ft.

### Walkway (CMC 304.4.2)

A solid floor a minimum of **24 in. wide** shall be provided from the access opening to the equipment.

### Work Platform (CMC 304.4.3)

A minimum **30 in. x 30 in. platform** shall be installed at the service area.

**Exception**: Equipment may be serviced from the access opening with a maximum 1 ft setback if serviced from the opening.

### Flooring (CMC 904.3)

Floor under furnace shall be of noncombustible construction.

**Exception**: When the appliance is listed for installation on a combustible floor.

### Lighting and Receptacle (CMC 304.4.4)

- A permanent 120V receptacle and light shall be located near the unit
- The switch controlling the lighting fixture shall be located at the access opening

### Attic Furnace Height (CMC 904.10)

Furnaces may be installed in an attic or furred space more than 5 ft in height, provided the required listings and furnace and duct clearances are observed.

---

## Crawl Space Installations

### Flood Elevation (CMC 305.3)

Appliance shall be installed above the design flood elevation.

### Access (CMC 304.4)

Same as attic: not less than 22 in. x 30 in. opening, 20 ft max passageway distance.

### Work Platform (CMC 304.4.3)

Minimum 30 in. x 30 in. level space on the service side.

### Support (CMC 904.3.1)

Appliance shall be supported on a concrete slab a minimum **3 in. above adjoining ground**, or suspended from floor framing above when installed per manufacturer's instructions and a minimum **6 in. above ground**.

### Excavation Clearances (CMC 305.2)

Where soil excavation is required:
- Minimum 6 in. below the appliance
- Minimum 12 in. on the sides
- Minimum 30 in. on the control side

Where excavation is greater than 12 in. below adjacent grade, walls shall be lined with concrete extending 4 in. above adjacent ground level.

### Lighting and Receptacle (CMC 304.4.4)

- Luminaire and receptacle outlet shall be provided adjacent to equipment
- Switch for luminaire shall be located at passageway entrance
- Exposed lamps shall be protected by placement or guards
- GFCI protection required for crawlspace lighting and receptacles (CEC 210.8)

## Quick Reference — Location Clearance Summary

| Location | Key Requirement | Code Section |
|----------|----------------|--------------|
| Bedroom/bathroom | Fuel-burning appliances prohibited | CMC 904.1 |
| Closet/alcove | Per manufacturer listing only; no clearance reduction | CMC 303.2 |
| Room (non-listed furnace) | Room volume >= 12x furnace volume | CMC 303.2 |
| Rooftop (slope >= 4:12) | 30 in. x 30 in. workspace on service side | CMC 304.2 |
| Rooftop (< 10 ft from edge) | 42 in. guardrail required | CMC 304.3.1.1 |
| Garage | Ignition source >= 18 in. above floor (unless FVIR) | CMC 305.1 |
| Attic | 22x30 opening, 24 in. walkway, 30x30 platform | CMC 304.4 |
| Crawl space | 3 in. above grade on slab, or 6 in. suspended | CMC 904.3.1 |
