Vent connector material and clearance: single-wall metal 6 in. to combustibles, Type B 1 in. to combustibles. | 2 | **Combustion air** | ❌ **fail** | Adequate air supply per option (single opening, two openings, indoor air, attic/crawl ducts); sizing per BTU input; duct material galvanized; exterior openings min 12 in above grade; louver free area. Granular duct requirements (CPC 701.11): ducts shall be of galvanized steel or equivalent corrosion-resistant material (701.11(1)); minimum duct size 3 in. diameter (701.11(2)); no dampers or louvers in combustion air ducts (701.11(3)); screens max 1/4 in. mesh (701.11(4)); vertical termination min 12 in. above grade (701.11(5)); horizontal ducts sized at 1 sq in. per 2,000 BTU/h each (701.11(6)); vertical ducts at 1 sq in. per 4,000 BTU/h each (701.11(7)).
