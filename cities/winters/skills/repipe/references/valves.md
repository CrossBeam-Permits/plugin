# CPC Section 606 — Valves

Source: 2025 California Plumbing Code (Title 24, Part 5), Chapter 6.

---

## Main Shutoff Valve (606.2)

| Requirement | Detail | Code Section |
|-------------|--------|-------------|
| **Location** | On the discharge side of the water meter | CPC 606.2 |
| **Type** | Fullway (gate or ball valve, full-port) | CPC 606.2 |
| **Bleed** | Shall have bleed orifice or separate drain | CPC 606.2 |
| **Accessibility** | Shall be accessible | CPC 606.2 |
| **Single-family exception** | A single-family residence and its accessory building may share one valve | CPC 606.3 |

---

## Water Heater Valve (606.2)

| Requirement | Detail | Code Section |
|-------------|--------|-------------|
| **Location** | On the cold water supply pipe to each water heater, at or near the heater | CPC 606.2 |
| **Type** | Fullway (gate or ball valve, full-port) | CPC 606.2 |
| **Shall not be throttled** | Main valve and water heater valve shall not be used as throttling valves | CPC 606.2 |

---

## Fixture and Appliance Control Valves (606.5)

| Requirement | Detail | Code Section |
|-------------|--------|-------------|
| **Control valve** | Shall be installed immediately ahead of each water-supplied appliance | CPC 606.5 |
| **Fixture supply** | Shall be installed immediately ahead of each slip joint or appliance supply | CPC 606.5 |
| **Accessibility** | Required shutoff or control valves shall be accessible | CPC 606.6 |

---

## Multi-Dwelling Units (606.3)

In multi-dwelling units, one or more shutoff valves shall be provided in each dwelling unit so that the water supply can be shut off without stopping water supply to other units. These valves shall be accessible in the dwelling unit they control.

---

## Unions Near Equipment (609.5)

Unions shall be installed in the water supply piping within **12 inches** of:
- Water heaters
- Water softeners
- Filters
- Regulators (PRVs)
- Similar equipment that may require removal for servicing

---

## Valve Material Requirements (606.1)

| Size | Material | Notes |
|------|----------|-------|
| Up to 2 in. | Copper alloy or other approved | NSF 14 dezincification-resistant if >15% zinc and used with plastic piping |
| Over 2 in. | Cast iron, copper alloy, or other approved | — |
| All sizes | Full-port / fullway | Gate or ball valve with non-corrosive working parts |
