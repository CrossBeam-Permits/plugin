A couple of things to flag before you submit:
First, the Cool Roof field is blank. California Energy Code (CZ 6) requires your shingles to meet a minimum aged reflectance of 0.20 + emittance of 0.75, or SRI ≥ 16. Standard GAF Timberline HDZ in darker colors may or may not qualify — check the product data sheet or pick from GAF's "Timberline HDZ Reflector Series" if you want to make sure. The city will want a C.R.R.C. number if you're checking that box.
Second, since you're going owner-builder, HB allows homeowners to pull permits on their own primary residence — but the city may ask you to sign an owner-builder declaration at the counter. Just confirm that when you go in or call ahead at (714) 536-5241.
